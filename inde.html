Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties does Appeal Point specialize in?

We specialize in commercial, multifamily, and industrial properties throughout Indiana. Our expertise spans warehouse facilities, retail centers, apartment complexes, office buildings, and specialized commercial properties.

How does the property tax appeal process work?

We begin with a comprehensive assessment review to identify opportunities for reduction. If we find merit for an appeal, we handle all aspects—from filing paperwork to negotiating settlements with local assessors or representing you at hearings. Our goal is maximum tax savings with minimal disruption to you.

What makes Appeal Point different from other property tax consultants?

Our team's unique background working inside the assessor's office combined with 35+ years of consulting experience means we understand both sides of the process. Our long-standing relationships within the midwest assessment community provides clients with access and advocacy others simply can't match.

When should I have my property assessment reviewed?

Ideally, assessments should be reviewed annually—before they become final. Proactive review allows us to address issues early and often avoid the need for formal appeals. We also recommend review after any major property changes, purchases, or market shifts.

How are your fees structured?

Our fee structure is typically contingency-based, meaning we only get paid when we achieve tax savings for you. We're happy to discuss fee arrangements during our initial consultation to find an approach that works best for your situation.

What areas of the Midwest do you serve?

Based in Indiana, we proudly support clients across the Midwest. Our regional presence gives us a deep understanding of local markets, regulations, and communities allowing us to deliver solutions that fit each client’s specific needs and unique environments.
